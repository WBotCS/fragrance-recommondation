Our Story

The idea for Scent Compass AI was inspired by my passion for fragrances, fascination with AI, and love for elegant UI design. Why not combine them all to create something truly innovative?

Details

This website leverages advanced AI algorithms to recommend the best fragrances tailored to your preferences. It collects user input through a detailed preferences form, which includes factors such as gender, preferred fragrance notes, and disliked notes. This data is then processed by our AI model, which has been trained on a vast dataset of fragrance profiles and user preferences. The model uses machine learning techniques to analyze patterns and correlations within the data, allowing it to make accurate and personalized fragrance recommendations. The training process involves continuous learning and refinement, ensuring that the model stays up-to-date with the latest trends and user feedback. By combining the power of AI with a user-friendly interface, we aim to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for finding your perfect scent.
